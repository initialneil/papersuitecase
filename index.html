Built for researchers

Every feature shaped around real academic workflows—no bloat, no learning curve.

arXiv Integration

Paste a URL,
get the paper

Drop any arXiv link into the search bar. Paper Suitecase fetches the PDF, extracts metadata—title, authors, abstract—and files it into your chosen entry and subfolder. It even flags similar papers you already have.

Tagging System

Tag papers
by project

Create hierarchical tags for each research project, reading group, or topic. Assign them in one click, filter your library instantly. Stop losing that one paper you read last month.

BibTeX Export

BibTeX ready
for Overleaf

Look up references from DBLP, generate BibTeX entries, and export them for your LaTeX projects. Manage citations per-tag so each project gets exactly the references it needs.

Your folders, your rules

Paper Suitecase never copies or moves your files. Create entries that link to folders on your disk—the same folders synced by your cloud provider.

Link, don't copy

Add any folder as an entry. PDFs stay exactly where they are—your existing file structure is preserved.

Cloud-sync friendly

Works with OneDrive, Dropbox, Nutstore, Google Drive—any service that syncs a folder. Your papers follow you across machines.

Auto-scan on launch

New PDFs dropped into your synced folder? Paper Suitecase detects additions, removals, and even renames automatically.

Less managing,
more reading

Free, open-source, and built for academics who just want their papers in one place.
